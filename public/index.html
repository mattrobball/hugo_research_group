Welcome to the SC AGNT group

We are a dynamic research group from the Department of Mathematics at the University of South Carolina. Our aim is to explore and understand questions dating back thousands of years.

We are grateful to acknowledge partial support from the the University of South Carolina, the Simons Foundation, the National Science Foundation, the National Security Agency, the Fields Institute, and the Southeastern Conference.
